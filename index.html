Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, helpful answers to common inquiries about working with MetaBiz Solutions.

What services does MetaBiz offer?

At MetaBiz, we specialize in high-impact Web Development, premium CMS/WordPress/Shopify platforms, custom desktop software architectures built in Python and C#, and cohesive creative designs including brand identities and full UI/UX visuals.

How long does it take to complete a web development project?

Timeline depends heavily on scope. Standard optimized web pages take between 2 to 4 weeks, whereas custom software integrations or large-scale digital platforms can take 4 to 8 weeks. We always present a firm delivery timeline during our discovery call.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Absolutely. We prioritize long-term partnerships. MetaBiz provides comprehensive ongoing support, periodic version maintenance, visual refreshes, and security audits to keep your systems running at peak capability.

Can I request custom features for my web or software project?

Yes. Custom features are our core competency. We build tailormade business pipelines, automation systems, database structures, and distinct visual components built exactly around your specific operational processes.

When and how do I pay?

For all projects, a 30-50% advance payment is required at the start to mobilize design and developer resources. The remaining amount is structured based on key project milestones or paid upon final successful delivery. We accept direct Bank Transfers, EasyPaisa, or JazzCash for your convenience.
